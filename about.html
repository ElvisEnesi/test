Pets stores

Pet store is a decentralize dynamic online website that serves as a medium for connecting individuals and wonderful creatures on the planet, it serves as an interface for helping people choose their right pet partner. Pet stores do not sell pets to people but connects them with store owners around the buyers location to provide them with their desired pet, pet store also connects it's users with the nearest vertinary clinic close by to help them treat their sick pets.
